Tiny but important reality check

Can it detect every piece of AI content?

NO.

Nobody can reliably identify every bit of unlabeled AI-generated text, imagery, or video from inside a browser. AI Slop Blocker catches explicit labels and known patterns. It will miss things, and it may occasionally hide something innocent. At least it is honest about it.
