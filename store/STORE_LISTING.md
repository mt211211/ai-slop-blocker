## Language

## Detailed description

Make the internet a little quieter.

AI Slop Blocker is a free Chrome extension that hides web content explicitly
labeled as AI-generated, AI-assisted, or synthetic. When it spots a matching
label or known AI interface pattern, it makes the relevant content invisible
while preserving the page layout as blank space.

Features:

- One clear on/off switch
- Automatically scans pages while enabled
- Handles content added after page load
- Restores hidden content immediately when switched off
- Runs entirely in your browser
- No analytics, accounts, or browsing-data collection

Important limitation:

No browser extension can reliably identify every piece of unlabeled
AI-generated text, image, or video. AI Slop Blocker detects explicit
disclosures, metadata, and known AI UI patterns. It will miss some content and
may occasionally hide a false positive.

## Single purpose

AI Slop Blocker's single purpose is to hide web content that is explicitly
labeled or identified by known page patterns as AI-generated, AI-assisted, or
synthetic.
